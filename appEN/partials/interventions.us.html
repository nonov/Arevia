Definition

What are we talking about?

"Coaching is the assistance given to a person (or a team) in a personal or professional situation, in order to reach its objectives and succeed his professional and personal life."

This is the relational and temporal space created by coaching sessions that make emerge solutions.

The coachee (or the team) is in its complexity and paradoxes.

Coaching is part of a sustainable development approach. Whether it is an individual or a group coaching (manager + team) it is necessary to consider the complexity of the overall system in which the individual or the team interact.

— And create a space of retreat and resources.

Team Building

With AREVIA the team building allows the executive committee or team managers to align their visions around a shared project.

Methodology ORT

ORT Model which I practice is a team coaching with three levels:

In its Operational life (its structure, functioning, rules...).

In moments of regulation (brake release).

In a training process: punctual contributions to increase skills.

This method helps to understand group processes, resolve relationship problems, decrease tensions and develop a stronger bond. It favors shared learning and welcomes co-creation.

Exemples of interventions :

Accompaniments on the teams intervene to deal with problem situations (such as lack of motivation, an important turnover, the growing aggressiveness, stress which is spreading, or the existence of simmering conflicts) or may be engaged to develop the autonomy or the initiative of a team.

Examples of requests :

Interventions on the teams could be to deal with issues such as demotivation, significant turnover, aggressivity, stress, frozen conflicts or in order to develop confidence, autonomy, initiatives, creativity, authenticity...

Intervention Rules

The rules raise the frame, protect both the coach, the coachee and the relationship, and allows the deployement of the coach and the coachee power.

Here are the main :

Contract

The coach looks at his client as a partner. It establishes with him an agreement stating the objective he wants to achieve and the context of intervention. Adjustments may be held as the progress of the intervention.

Process

The coach is an expert in the coaching process.

Understanding of stakes

His partner role requires to understand the business challenges of its customers without being an expert in the job of its client. The coach will ask the right questions, the client will find the answers. The client has the entire responsibility of the decisions made.

Confidentiality

The coach is bound by professional secrecy.

Refusal of care

The coach may refuse to take over coaching for organization-specific reasons, to the applicant or to himself. He asks in this case one of his colleagues.

Coach must refuse to help the client to act outside of the country laws or if the intervention does not ensure respect for people.

Responsibility for decisions

Coaching is a professional and personal technical development. The coach give to the coachee the responsability to decide.

Personal Protection

The coach adapts its interventions in respect of the stages of development of his client.

Field of practice

The coach must inform the client when the issues raised by the intervention are beyond his capacities and recommend the appropriate use of other professionals.

Protection of the organization

The coach is attentive to the job, customs, and cultures of the company which he is working with. He considers the customer (or group of people) as part of an overall system and acts in the interests of the entire system.

Restitution

The coach can account for its actions to the client only within the limits established with the coachee.

Remedy

The coach must have a place of supervision and therapy when the client’s issue brings back the coach’s professional difficulties.

Training

The coach must be able to explain to the client the theoretical foundations of the accompanying processes used and secondly he must informs and train himself to advanced research in professional fields of its competence.

Methodology

Fish or fishing rod?

It’s depending on the context, the urgency, the degree of development of the individual or the team, its level of autonomy or maturity to face the situation :

My intervention will orient either towards a supply of fish, which means diagnostic elements, action options, ideas, or confrontations.

Or it will be to accompany the constitution of the fishing rod, which means to develop their autonomy, develop its own decisions, validate their own ideas, strengthen their confidence and self-esteem, better perceive the stakes , get out of confusion, identify priorities, get out of the paradoxes...
